## Business Requirements

The objective of this project is to analyze Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction and product distribution using SQL.

### KPI Requirements

- Total Sales
- Average Sales
- Number of Items
- Average Rating

### Analysis Requirements

1. Analyze total sales by item fat content.
2. Analyze total sales by item type.
3. Compare sales by fat content across different outlet locations.
4. Analyze total sales based on outlet establishment year.
5. Calculate the percentage of sales by outlet size.
6. Analyze sales performance across different outlet locations.
7. Compare total sales, average sales, number of items and average ratings by outlet type.
